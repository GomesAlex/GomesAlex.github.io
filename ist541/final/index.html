Why Are Fountain Pens Still a Thing?

The fountain pen may conjure antique images of a writing instrument that is obsolete in contemporary society. These types of pens aren't a necessity; however, the idea of writing with a well-made, highly customizable pen that lays down ink of an infinite number of colors is a truly unique experience.

This site was created by brainstorming on a pad of paper sitting at the kitchen table. I find the absence of technology aids in calming my mind and focusing on the topic at hand. Additionally, fountain pens provide an eco-friendly option since you can refill your pen without disposing of it as you would a standard ballpoint. As a hobby, there are fountain pen shows worldwide, social clubs, and online communities to enjoy the form and function of pens and inks that go with them. Without further ado, please enjoy perusing the pages of this site, and delve into the world of fountain pens with me.
